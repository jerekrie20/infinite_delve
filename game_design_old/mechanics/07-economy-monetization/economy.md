# 07 · Economy & Monetization

The daily-engagement dial + how money is made — without pay-to-win.

## Decided ✅ (2026-07-14)
- **No energy cap — unlimited personal [[delve-generation|delves]] per day.** The
  daily habit comes from the **Daily Delve** + [[frontier]] progress + the
  [[reddit-native]] report, not an energy timer.
- **The Daily Delve** runs **1× per day** (possibly weekly — see below).
- **Currency ladder:** **copper → silver → gold → diamond** (escalating
  denominations; earned from delves, spent in the town shop).

## Proposed (still open)
- **Monetization = cosmetics + convenience** (gear skins, capstone flair, pets,
  banner emblems, maybe extra stash). **Never** power, **never** paid delves.
  *(Boundary = FINALIZE #4, still deferred by you.)*
- **Generosity-as-status:** a coin-funded **community supply drop** boosting the
  sub's frontier push, buyer publicly credited (blueprint idea).
- Ties to Reddit Developer Funds (daily engagers + installs) as primary revenue;
  Reddit Gold secondary.

## ❓ To finalize
- Daily Delve cadence — **daily** (stronger engagement) or **weekly** (more of
  an event)? *(I'd lean daily for the daily-engager payout.)*
- Is **diamond** an earned top denomination, or the **premium** (Reddit-Gold)
  currency?
- Monetization boundary (#4): cosmetics-only, or convenience too? Keep the
  supply drop?

# World & Naming Bible — tone, fiction, and what things are called

The words-side companion to [[ART_BIBLE]]. Keeps 60 abilities, 33 bases,
sets, uniques, monsters, and every Reddit post sounding like ONE game.

> **Scope split (D50):** WORLD owns the VOICE, tone, and naming rules.
> **[[LORE]] owns the fiction content** — the hidden truth ("It digs
> back"), every fragment (bestiary lines, item flavor, the 20 encounters),
> the season arc, and secret-deed hints. Write in WORLD's voice, obey
> LORE's canon.

## Tone

**Lantern-lit grit (aligned to the grim-glow art, D29).** The world is
dark and the work is dangerous; the people doing it are weathered, wry,
and proud. Humor exists but it's miners' humor — dry, understated, earned
— never slapstick, never cute. Stakes are played straight: the Delve
takes people's hauls and sometimes their nerve, and the Guild goes back
down anyway. That defiance IS the brand. Think veteran foreman reading
the day's report by lamplight, not doom metal and not a storybook.
Violence is dark-fantasy stylized — flashes and shadows, never gore
(all-ages Reddit surface).

## The fiction (one paragraph, canonical)

Beneath the lantern-lit surface town of **Torchrest**, the ground opened
into the **Delve** — a bottomless, shifting deep that rearranges itself
every day and coughs up treasure the deeper you go. Torchrest's
**Delvers' Guild** (that's the subreddit) sends heroes down on the lift.
Extraction is riding the lift back up with your haul; dying just means
the Delve keeps your bags (the lift always brings YOU back). Every season
the Delve births a new **Terror** rising toward the surface — the Guild's
frontier war ([[frontier]]) is beating the Terror's brood back down before
season's end.

Canon anchors: the lift (extract animation), Torchrest (base camp
backdrop, shop flavor), the Guild = the sub, Terrors = frontier ladder
bosses, "the Delve rearranges daily" = daily seed.

## Naming conventions (per content type)

| Type | Pattern | Examples (canon) |
|------|---------|------------------|
| Depth themes | evocative place, 1-2 words | Goblin Camp, Crypt, Warrens, Deep, Volcanic*, Abyss (*rename candidate: "the Cinderways") |
| Monsters | plain Adjective+Noun, readable at a glance | Goblin Scout, Plague Rat, Null Witch |
| Bosses | "The X" or Name+Title | The Hollow King, Pyre Tyrant, Herald of the Abyss |
| Frontier Terrors | escalating menace names, seasonal | e.g. "The Gnawing", "Mother of Teeth" |
| Sets | Possessive or compound + noun | Warden's Vigil, Cindersworn, Voidbound |
| Uniques | two evocative words, no "of the" chains | Gutripper, Everburn, Null Sigil |
| Abilities | verb-noun or two punchy words | Crushing Blow, Hunter's Mark, Absolute Zero |
| Gear bases | one plain material-free noun | Sword, Circlet, Quiver (tiers add Crude/Fine/Mythic) |
| Classes | rank words that ladder | Squire→Warrior→Knight; Archer→Ranger→Sniper; Apprentice→Mage→Archmage |

Rules: no apostrophe-heavy fantasy gibberish (no "Zar'keth"); names must
read at pixel-font sizes; every proper noun gets ONE canonical spelling
recorded here or in its catalog.

## Reddit post voice ([[reddit-native]])

Written as **Guild ledger entries from Torchrest's foreman** — terse,
weathered, proud of the crew, names real players. Short sentences. One
emoji max per title. Templates:

- Daily report: "⛏️ Guild Ledger, Day {n}. The crews drove {damage} deep.
  {topPlayer} went furthest — {depth} — and came back. The {terror} felt
  it."
- Boss felled: "The {terror} is DOWN. Every lamp in Torchrest is lit
  tonight. The ledger remembers {top3} — and everyone who swung. The lift
  goes deeper tomorrow."
- Season finale: win = grim satisfaction ("We dug it out. Rest. The Delve
  will grow another."); loss = defiance ("The {terror} reached the gates.
  Torchrest holds. Next season we dig angrier.")

Never corporate, never system-speak ("engagement", "rewards program"),
never shame low contributors, never break the weathered-pride register
for a meme.
